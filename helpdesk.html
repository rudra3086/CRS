Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a complaint?
To submit a complaint, log in to your account and click on the "New Complaint" button in the dashboard. Fill out the complaint form with all necessary details and submit it. You'll receive a confirmation email with your complaint reference number.
How can I track my complaint status?
You can track your complaint status by visiting the "Track Complaint" page and entering your complaint reference number. Alternatively, log in to your account to view all your complaints and their current status.
What are the response time expectations?
We aim to respond to all complaints within 24-48 hours. Complex issues may take longer to resolve. You'll receive regular updates on your complaint status through email notifications.
How do I escalate my complaint?
If you're not satisfied with the resolution or if your complaint hasn't been addressed within the expected timeframe, you can escalate it by clicking the "Escalate" button on your complaint details page or by contacting our support team.
